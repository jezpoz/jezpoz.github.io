About

As an employee, I was tasked with modernizing the existing websites. Our old websites were made in WordPress. Don't get me wrong, there is nothing wrong with WordPress as long as it works. I personally find it a bit "outdated". But that is just my personal opinion.

So we made a plan to modernize the webpage with Sanity CMS, Next.js for server-side rendering and all the SEO goodies that comes with it.
